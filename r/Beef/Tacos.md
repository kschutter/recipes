# Tacos

######  Yield: ?
######  Time: 30 min

##  *Ingredients*
- 1 lb lean ground beef
- 5 green onions
- 1/2 green pepper
- 3 large garlic cloves
<!---->
- 1 package taco seasoning
- salt to taste

##  *Directions*
1. Brown meat with garlic, onion and pepper

2. Add taco seasoning mix and 2/3 cup of water

3. Simmer for 4 minutes
