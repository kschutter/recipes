# Cherry-Almond Smoothie

######  Yield: 2 servies
######  Time:  5 minutes

##  *Ingredients*
- banana, (sliced)
- 2 cups frozen pitted cherries
- 1 cup almond milk
- 1/2 cup low-fat yogurt
- 1 to 2 tablespoons honey

##  *Directions*
1. Combine in a blender and puree until smooth. Adjust sweetness with additional honey.
